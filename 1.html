Mushoku Tensei

The main story of Mushoku Tensei focuses on a 34-year-old man who was killed by a

truck and then reincarnated as a baby named Rudeus Greyrat who lives in a world

filled with swords and magic. He is a baby who grows into a teenager who is

proficient in magic and becomes a powerful heir to magic in his family.
