Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen follows the story of Yuji Itadori, an ordinary boy

who crosses paths with Megumi Fushiguro, a Jujutsu Sorcerer

searching for a powerful Cursed Object known as Ryomen Sukuna's

finger. Unintentionally, Yuji's friends unseal the Cursed Object,

attracting dangerous Curses to their location.
