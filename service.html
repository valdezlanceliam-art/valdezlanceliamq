HORIZON ANIME SITE

Horizon was made out of a profound passion for anime and a strong

desire to cultivate a thriving community around this incredible art

form. As an avid fan, I wanted to establish a space where fellow

enthusiasts could come together to celebrate and explore the vast

and diverse world of anime.


Horizon aims to contribute to the wider recognition of anime as a

powerful storytelling medium, showcasing its artistic brilliance and

its impact on global pop culture. Ultimately, this website is my way

of sharing my passion for anime and creating a welcoming space where

fans can immerse themselves in the enchanting world of anime.
