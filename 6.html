Freiren Beyond Journey's End

It follows an elf mage, Frieren, after she and her companions defeat the Demon King

and go back to their regular lives. Except Frieren is near-immortal,

and the rest of the party is human. She spends the next fifty years off exploring and learning more magic...

while her former companions age and approach death.
